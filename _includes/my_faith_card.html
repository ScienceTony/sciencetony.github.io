Protestantism and Baptists

The Baptist Church is a major branch of Protestantism, which is the second largest form of Christianity and accounts for about 40% of all Christians.

Baptism and Alpha groups

Baptists believe in baptisms of full submersion. Giving a short testimony before being baptised is traditional. If a member of the public wishes to learn more about being a Christian and being baptised, they attend Alpha group meeting over a period of weeks in order to decide if it is what they really want to do.
